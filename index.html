LATEST PROJECTS

    SHORT FILM: Cockpit Portal

    Cockpit Portal is a short film serving as a visual teaser/opening to an extended magical-realist, action tale about a strong-spirited, obstinate young maroon shaken by his time as a slave travelling throughout the Jamaica Cockpit Country, across nearby plantations, and along the very continuum of space-time itself in his quest to rescue his sibling. He must use his keenly acquired knowledge of the terrain and indigenous forestry to evasively traverse the mountainous landscape, with the confidence that his tactics will shroud him from being discovered and detained. The story is propelled by themes of 'time and change', 'family and heritage', 'courage and perseverance' and 'redemption'.


    ART INSTALLATION: Where will the Bauxite / Baxide / BS [Box-Site] end?

    enDemic JA is a sole proprietorship, founded and certified with the Companies Office of Jamaica in May 2021. One of its main objectives is to create ecologically centered art, with a view to advocating climate and environmental justice.


    The bauxite topic is juxtaposed with boxes as a question and statement to how the product of erosion/mining materialises; see uses of aluminium. What is gained and what is lost? What views might arise in our efforts to quantify the impact of this industry on the environment?


    This "box-site" not only welcomes symbolic play, but uses red dirt, live flora (sweet potato vine, or colloquially: fire inna earth), and raw/unrefined surfaces/materials to ask viewers to pay attention to what is being laid bare.
